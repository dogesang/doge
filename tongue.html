Appreciate flowers, you must understand flower language
which constitutes the core of flower culture.
In flower communication, although flower language is silent, it is better than sound at this time.
The meaning and emotional expression in it are more than words.
The result will only lead to misunderstandings by others.

The flower language of Violet is "eternal beauty and love, simplicity and virtue".
Violets are not only purple flowers, but also purple, light red, light yellow, blue, and white.
Different colors of violets have different flower language.
The flower language of blue violets: vigilance, loyalty, I will always be loyal.
Blue Violet has a very mysterious name-Demon Eye.

The flower language of sunflower is behavior, brilliance, arrogance, loyalty, and admiration.
The meaning of sunflower is the love of cotton.
Sunflower represents the pursuit of one's own happiness in a simulated manner.
But it is sunny, bright, love is magnanimous, love is inseparable,
it belongs to its own unique charm, and its blooming enthusiasm is love, as well as love for dreams and life.

Carnation flower language: passion, charm, tenderness, true love,
mother I love you, warm blessing, love you, kindness, maternal love does not ask for a price,
tolerance, mother's flower, deep affection, affection and yearning,
pure love, passion, enthusiasm , Sorrow and regret, greatness, sacred,
mourning, heart communication, sincerity, luck, longing.

Tulip flower language: fraternity, thoughtfulness, elegance,
wealth, charity, reputation, beauty, blessing,
eternity, love confession, eternal blessing.
